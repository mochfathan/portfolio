Model v3 mechanics: Gross income → income tax → living costs = net disposable. Buyer and renter each apply their own investment allocation % to free cash flow (disposable − housing cost). Owner recurring costs are decomposed into property tax, insurance, maintenance, capex reserve, HOA/condo fees, and owner utilities premium. Both portfolios compound monthly via price appreciation + DRIP-adjusted dividends. Annual bonus lump sum added once/year. Emergency fund is pre-funded from initial capital and held outside investment portfolio. Capital gains tax on gains above cumulative cost basis at exit. Buyer equity = home value − mortgage balance − selling costs. Relocation: buyer liquidates home at specified year; net proceeds added to portfolio; buyer thereafter pays rent.
